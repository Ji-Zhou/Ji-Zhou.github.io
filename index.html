About Me

I am pursuing Ph.D under the supervision of Prof. Nan Zheng in the Institute of Transport Engineering, Department of Civil Engineering. Prior to that, I obtained my B.Eng. from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications under the supervision of Prof. Senyan Yang, specializing in Postal Engineering (Internet and Intelligent Logistics), a subfield of Automation.

My research focuses on advancing intelligent transportation systems, particularly in freight transportation and logistics, leveraging model-driven, search-driven, and data-driven methodologies. I am also keen on integrating simulation, verification, and software development to enhance the practicality and innovation of my work, ensuring both academic and industrial relevance.
