About Me

I am a first-year bachelor-straight-to-Ph.D student under the supervision of Prof. Nan Zheng in the Institute of Transport Engineering, Department of Civil Engineering. Prior to joining Monash, I earned my Bachelor of Engineering (B.Eng.) from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, specializing in Postal Engineering (Internet and Intelligent Logistics), a subfield of Automation.

My research focuses on advancing intelligent transportation systems (ITS), particularly in freight transportation and logistics, leveraging model-driven, search-driven, and data-driven methodologies. I am also keen on integrating simulation, verification, and software development to enhance the practicality of my work, ensuring both academic rigor and industrial relevance.
